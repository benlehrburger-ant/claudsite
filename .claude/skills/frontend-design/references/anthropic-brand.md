## Typography

### Primary Typefaces

- **Anthropic Sans** — Proprietary. Display (48px+), Text (16px and below), variable optical sizing for intermediate sizes.
- **Anthropic Mono** — Proprietary monospaced for code and technical content.

### Fallback Stacks

- Sans: `"System-UI", "Segoe UI", Roboto, Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif`
- Mono: `Menlo, Monaco, monospace`
- Non-Latin: Google Noto font family

### Type Rules

- Avoid title case and all-caps
- Tabular figures for numeric data
- Ligatures enabled
- Optical sizing: match optical size axis to font size (e.g., 32px font = 32 optical size)

## Illustration Principles

- **Economy of line** — Minimal strokes with clear communicative purpose
- **Elemental expression** — Complex ideas distilled to tangible, foundational forms
- **Human touch** — Subtle irregularities and organic shapes over sterile perfection
- Clarity and impact at all scales
- Never overload compositions or obscure meaning

## Logo Types

- Anthropic wordmark, icon, partner lockups
- Claude wordmark, spark, icon, signature
- "Claude by Anthropic" and "Powered by Claude" lockups
- Each has defined clearspace and color rules
